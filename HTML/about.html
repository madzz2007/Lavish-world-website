Our Story

Lavish Home Interior is a premium interior design and decoration company based in Nanganallur, Chennai. We specialize in transforming residential and commercial spaces into elegant, functional, and personalized environments.

With years of experience and a passionate design team, we have successfully completed projects across India, including homes, offices, studios, hotels, and retail spaces. Our goal is to combine creativity, quality, and comfort to deliver interiors that truly reflect our clients' lifestyles and personalities.
